Software Engineering

Taught by Dr. Marcus Henderson

An introduction to the principles and practices of software engineering, covering the full software development lifecycle. Topics include requirements gathering, system design, agile methodologies, version control, testing strategies, and deployment. Students will work in teams to design and build a real-world software project from inception to delivery.
